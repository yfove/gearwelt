The launch of the **Apple Vision Pro** marks the beginning of the spatial computing era. Designed to seamlessly blend digital content with the physical world, this standalone headset introduces a three-dimensional interface controlled by the most natural and intuitive inputs possible: a user’s eyes, hands, and voice. At its core, the Vision Pro utilizes a custom dual-chip design featuring the **M2 processor** for standalone performance and the all-new **R1 chip**, specifically engineered to process input from 12 cameras, five sensors, and six microphones with a virtually lag-free **12-millisecond latency**.

The visual experience is centered around two ultra-high-resolution **micro-OLED displays** that pack a combined 23 million pixels—delivering more than 4K resolution to each eye. This density ensures that text remains sharp and immersive environments feel remarkably lifelike. To maintain a sense of presence, the **EyeSight** feature uses a curved external OLED panel to reveal the user's eyes to those nearby, signaling when the user is engaged with apps or fully immersed in an experience.

Crafted with premium materials, the headset features a singular piece of three-dimensionally formed laminated glass that flows into a custom aluminum alloy frame. The modular design includes a **Solo Knit Band** and a **Dual Loop Band**, allowing users to find a precise and comfortable fit. For audio, the Vision Pro utilizes dual-driver audio pods positioned next to each ear to deliver **Personalized Spatial Audio**, which creates the sensation that sounds are coming from the environment around the user.

Whether used for professional multitasking with Mac Virtual Display or for entertainment on a screen that feels 100 feet wide, the Apple Vision Pro provides a glimpse into the future of personal technology. It is a masterful integration of high-performance hardware and the new **visionOS**, offering an infinite canvas for those looking to redefine their relationship with digital workspaces.
