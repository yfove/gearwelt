Draped in a 4-layer aluminum and BMC chassis with a 10mm brushed top plate, it wields a 3.6kg brass-topped platter for flawless rotation. Fresh upgrades include a Hybrid Encoder for rock-steady pitch and a low-noise switching power supply, ensuring every groove sings true. Gold-painted tonearm, logoed headshell, and a serialized Master Edition badge elevate its collector’s allure, while eco-friendly cardboard packaging honors the planet.
