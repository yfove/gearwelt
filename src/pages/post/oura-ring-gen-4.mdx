The **Oura Ring Gen 4** reaffirms Oura’s position as the leader in the smart ring category, delivering a masterclass in miniaturization and data accuracy. In an era where wearable tech often feels bulky or intrusive, the Gen 4 manages to pack medical-grade sensors into a sleek, titanium band that looks and feels like traditional jewelry.

The most significant upgrade in this generation is the move to **Smart Sensing technology**. While previous models featured raised sensor bumps that could occasionally cause discomfort, the Gen 4 features a **completely smooth, recessed interior**. This isn't just for comfort; the new sensing platform utilizes over 18 signal pathways to dynamically choose the best way to read your biometrics, significantly improving accuracy across different finger shapes and skin tones.

Beyond the hardware, the Gen 4 introduces a revamped app experience centered around long-term wellness. From its legendary **Sleep Score** and **Readiness Score** to new features like Daytime Stress tracking and Cardiovascular Age, the ring provides a holistic view of your health without the distraction of a screen. With an improved battery life of up to **8 days** and a vast array of finishes—including a new, high-polish Silver and Brushed Silver—the Oura Ring Gen 4 remains the gold standard for those who want to track everything while wearing nothing.
