The **Breville Barista Express Impress** represents a significant step forward for the home espresso enthusiast, focusing on solving the two most difficult variables in brewing: consistent dosing and tamping. By integrating the **Impress™ Puck System**, Breville has created a machine that offers the tactile satisfaction of a manual espresso press with the precision of automated technology. This system utilizes a smart dosing mechanism that automatically calculates the required amount of coffee, while the assisted tamping lever delivers a consistent **10kg (22lb) of pressure** and a professional 7-degree barista twist.

Performance is anchored by a high-quality integrated **conical burr grinder**, featuring 25 distinct grind settings to cater to various bean profiles. The machine’s intelligent dosing system learns as you use it, automatically adjusting the grind duration to ensure you hit the perfect level in your filter basket. This feedback loop is visualized through a precise gauge on the front panel, which confirms when the "ideal" dose and tamp have been achieved, virtually eliminating the mess and waste common in traditional setups.

Temperature and pressure management are handled with typical Breville precision. A **Thermocoil heating system** with PID control ensures water is delivered at exactly 93°C (200°F), while the low-pressure pre-infusion gradually increases pump pressure to ensure a uniform extraction. For those who prioritize milk texture, the powerful manual steam wand produces high-velocity steam to create the microfoam required for silk-smooth lattes and intricate latte art.
