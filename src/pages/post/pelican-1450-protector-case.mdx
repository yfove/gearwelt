The **Pelican 1450 Protector Case** remains a definitive choice for professionals and enthusiasts who prioritize the safety of their most sensitive equipment. Whether you are transporting radio gear, specialized outdoor tools, or a delicate GoPro and lens kit, this case offers a level of security that few competitors can match. Its ultra-hardwearing polypropylene body is designed to withstand extreme impact, serving as a practically indestructible fortress for your cargo.

Measuring **16.44” x 13” x 6.82”**, the 1450 is sized for versatility without sacrificing portability. The case utilizes a sophisticated sealing system, featuring an ABS latch and a polymer O-ring that creates a watertight barrier. This construction allows the case to meet rigorous industrial and military standards, including **IP67, MIL C-4150J, and Def Stan 81-41** ratings, ensuring protection against dust, water immersion, and heavy vibrations.

Beyond its crushproof exterior, the 1450 is engineered for buoyancy. It is capable of floating in water even when fully loaded, provided the total weight of the gear does not exceed **30 lbs**. This makes it an essential companion for maritime use or unpredictable outdoor environments where gear recovery is a vital consideration.

With its 2024 release, the Pelican 1450 Protector Case reinforces its reputation for reliability. It combines heavy-duty engineering with thoughtful design, providing a premium storage solution that guarantees peace of mind for anyone carrying high-value equipment into the field.
