The **Clicks Creator Keyboard** is a unique accessory that transforms the iPhone into a tactile productivity tool. By moving the keyboard off the screen and into a physical case, it solves the long-standing issue of virtual keyboards covering half of your content. This is a specialized tool built for writers, power users, and anyone who misses the precision of a physical button.

The primary benefit of Clicks is the reclaimed screen space. Because the iOS virtual keyboard stays hidden, you gain nearly 50% more room for apps like Notes, Slack, or Instagram. The keys themselves are designed with a satisfying clicky response and include a full row of iOS shortcuts, allowing you to use Cmd+Space for search or Cmd+H to return home just like on a Mac.

Power management is handled seamlessly through a direct connection. The case plugs into the iPhone’s USB-C or Lightning port, meaning it never needs a separate battery or a Bluetooth pairing process. It also supports passthrough charging, so you can keep your phone topped up without removing the case. For creators who prioritize speed and screen real estate, Clicks offers a functional throwback to a more tactile era of mobile computing.
