The **DJI Mini 4 Pro** is the ultimate expression of "big things come in small packages." Weighing in at under **249 grams**, it bypasses the need for registration in many regions while offering a suite of features previously reserved for drones twice its size and price. It is arguably the most capable "travel drone" ever created.

Safety is the standout upgrade for this generation. The Mini 4 Pro introduces **Omnidirectional Obstacle Sensing**, utilizing four wide-angle vision sensors and two downward sensors to detect obstacles in all directions. This allows even novice pilots to fly with confidence in complex environments. This safety net is paired with **ActiveTrack 360°**, enabling the drone to perform sophisticated, cinematic tracking maneuvers while autonomously navigating around trees, buildings, and terrain.

On the imaging front, the drone excels with a 1/1.3-inch CMOS sensor capable of **4K/60fps HDR video** and 48MP RAW photos. For social media enthusiasts, the **True Vertical Shooting** feature physically rotates the gimbal 90 degrees, capturing high-quality portrait video without losing resolution to cropping. With a flight time of up to 34 minutes (or 45 with the Intelligent Flight Battery Plus), the DJI Mini 4 Pro ensures you have enough airtime to capture the perfect shot every time.
